Visit Request Feature

Bridge Digital Discovery with Offline Relationships

Transform digital touchpoints into meaningful offline relationships that drive trust, education, and increased orders

The Visit Request Feature bridges the digital-physical gap in Indian B2B retail by enabling buyers to request in-person assistance from brand field executives directly through the Badho platform. This feature replicates the trusted offline sales model where retailers can call upon distributors' sales teams for product education, business guidance, and relationship building.

In Indian retail, relationships drive transactions. Retailers, especially in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities, rely heavily on personal relationships with sales executives.

How the Complete System Works

From digital discovery to offline relationship building - a seamless 6-step process

1

Discovery Phase

Buyer browses brands on Badho platform, encounters products they're interested in but want guidance on, and sees "Request Sales Executive Visit" option.

2

Request Initiation

Buyer clicks request button (available on brand pages, seller catalogs, product details). System captures buyer location and brand interest with timestamp.

3

Intelligent Routing

System checks if brand has field executive mapped to buyer's pincode. If available: immediate WhatsApp notification. If not: routed to internal calling team.

4

Smart Notifications

Immediate notification during business hours (9am-9pm). Outside hours: queued for next business day. Daily reminders until completion.

5

Visit Execution

Field executive visits buyer at their store, provides comprehensive brand education and support, builds relationship and assesses opportunity.

6

Completion Verification

Location capture when marking complete, simple web-based completion system, distance tracking for brand visibility and analytics.

The Digital Opportunity: Inbound vs Outbound Sales

📊 Traditional Field Sales Challenge

Your field executives visit 10-12 shopkeepers daily using their own judgment and local intelligence, achieving roughly 50% conversion rates. This is outbound sales - visiting random prospects hoping to find interest.

Comprehensive FAQ

Do our field executives need to install any app?
No. Everything works through WhatsApp and simple web links.
What if a retailer requests a visit but we don't service that area?
The request routes to our calling team who will assist and inform you.
How do we handle field executive leaves/absences?
Backup executive assignments ensure continuous coverage.
Can we track visit outcomes and ROI?
Yes, comprehensive analytics track visits, conversions, and business impact.
What if buyer is not available during visit?
Field executive can reschedule and system tracks follow-up attempts.
How does the location tracking work?
When marking a visit complete, the system captures the executive's location and maps it against the buyer's shop location to show brands the distance between them.
Can we stop the feature if it's not working for us?
Yes, it can be disabled anytime with 24-hour notice.
What's the cost for participating brands?
No additional cost - included as part of standard Badho partnership.
