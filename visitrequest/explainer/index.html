Frequently Asked Questions

Do our field executives need to install any app?
No. Everything works through WhatsApp and simple web links.
What if a retailer requests a visit but we don't service that area?
The request routes to our calling team who will assist and inform you.
Can we stop the feature if it's not working for us?
Yes, it can be disabled anytime with 24-hour notice.
How do we know if a visit actually happened?
Location tracking shows where the executive was when marking complete, plus timestamp verification.
What's the cost?
No additional cost - included as value-added service for Badho brand partners.
