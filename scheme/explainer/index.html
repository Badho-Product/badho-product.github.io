Badho Schemes Guide

Instant Benefits for Better Conversions

Two Simple Scheme Types

Volume Discount

When order value crosses a set amount, buyer gets:

  • Percentage off (like 10%)
  • Flat rupee off (like ₹100)
  • Percentage with cap (like 10% up to ₹100)
  • Optional free item as reward

Free Product

When buyer purchases X units, they get:

  • Y units free of same item
  • Z units free of different item
  • Cross-selling opportunities
  • Sampling for future orders
